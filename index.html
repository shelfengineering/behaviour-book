What does it do?

📝
10-second logging
Tap student, positive or negative, pick a category. Done. Designed for the pace of a real classroom.
📊
Automatic patterns
The app flags recurring behaviours, hotspot locations, and students whose positive ratio drops below target.
🎯
Tiered next steps
Students are auto-classified into Tier 1/2/3. Each tier gets specific, evidence-based strategy suggestions.
🔔
Follow-up reminders
Schedule follow-ups when you log. The app surfaces overdue items and nudges you about quiet Tier 2/3 students.
📈
Analytics & reports
Category charts, time-of-day patterns, day-of-week trends, and a printable weekly summary for leadership.
🤝
Multiple frameworks
SWPBS, Berry Street, Restorative Practices, or your own school values. Pick during setup, customise anytime.
